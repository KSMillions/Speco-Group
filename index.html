About Speco Group

We build the systems behind the structure.

Speco Group is a South African construction optimization and systems integration consultancy, purpose-built for the Built Environment industry. We are a founder-led firm, established on the principle that great construction businesses are built with intelligent systems and precise processes.

Built by a practising Contracts Manager with 6+ years of on-site experience at leading tier-one firms (WBHO, Raubex, VDLV) and landmark projects including the South African Reserve Bank (SARB) Headquarters. We possess deep regulatory knowledge across JBCC, GCC 2015, CIDB, SACPCMP, OHS Act, and SANS standards.

We do not offer generic software packages. We bridge the gap between hard-hat construction expertise and high-performance technology — solving operational problems construction companies didn’t know could be solved digitally.
