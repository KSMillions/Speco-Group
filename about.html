Our Story

We build the systems
behind the structure.

Speco Group is a founder-led Systems Optimization and Workflow Automation consultancy, established on the principle that great businesses — in any industry — are built with intelligent systems and precise processes.

We began life at the intersection of hard-hat site management and high-performance technology. Our founder's 6+ years of on-site experience at leading tier-one firms — including WBHO, Raubex, and VDLV — and landmark projects including the South African Reserve Bank (SARB) Headquarters gave us something no software firm can replicate: the operational DNA of real high-stakes environments.

We possess deep regulatory and compliance knowledge across JBCC, GCC 2015, CIDB, SACPCMP, OHS Act, and SANS standards — frameworks that taught us what precision, accountability, and zero-failure tolerance truly demand.

Today, that same operational rigour underpins every workflow automation system, document pipeline, and digital tool we build — for any industry that cannot afford operational failure.
